Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile applications.

01

Information We Collect

Our games may collect limited information automatically, including:

02

Advertising

Our games may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

For more info, review Google's Privacy Policy.

03

Data Usage

Collected data is used for:

  • Showing advertisements

04

Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will remove the information promptly.

05

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Data transmitted through third-party services is encrypted.

06

Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services including but not limited to:

These services may collect information per their own privacy policies.

07
